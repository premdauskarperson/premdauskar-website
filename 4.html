Dua Lipa Career Information

Early Career

Dua Lipa began her music career at the age of 14 by posting covers of her favorite songs on YouTube. She was influenced by artists such as Nelly Furtado and Pink, and her online presence eventually caught the attention of music executives.

Breakthrough and Debut Album

In 2015, she signed with Warner Music Group and released her first single, "New Love". Her self-titled debut studio album, released in 2017, included hit singles such as "Be the One", "IDGAF", and the international success "New Rules", which became her first number-one single in the UK.

Continued Success and "Future Nostalgia"

Following the success of her debut album, Dua Lipa released her second album, "Future Nostalgia", in 2020. The album was critically acclaimed and featured hits like "Don't Start Now", "Physical", and "Levitating". It won several awards and solidified her status as a global pop icon.

Awards and Recognition

Lipa has received numerous accolades, including Grammy Awards, Brit Awards, and MTV Europe Music Awards. She is known for her mezzo-soprano vocal range and her distinctive style that blends pop with disco, house, and R&B influences.

Collaborations and Other Ventures

Throughout her career, Dua Lipa has collaborated with various artists, including Calvin Harris, Sean Paul, and Elton John. She has also ventured into modeling and has become a prominent figure in the fashion industry.
